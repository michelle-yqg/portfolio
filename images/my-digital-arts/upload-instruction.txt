In this folder, you can upload the pictures in the digital art page.

The images should have an extension ".jpg" and should be named between number 1 to 30.
For example, "1.jpg", "2.jpg", "3.jpg"...The extension cannot be capitalized ".JPG".
The smaller number shows up at the top left of the page and the following images are going to
be placed toward right. After every three images, the next image is placed under the first image.
